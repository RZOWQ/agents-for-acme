ACME Corp Media Case Study 
Company Overview 
ACME Corp is a prominent player in the media industry, with an extensive collection of audio and video content that comprises podcasts, interviews, news broadcasts, and documentaries. Their success in delivering premium content to a diverse audience requires a content management system that can keep pace with the dynamic media landscape. 
Solution Concept 
ACME Corp seeks to modernize its content management and user engagement strategies using Google Cloud's generative AI. They want a platform that empowers customers with personalized recommendations, natural language interactions, and seamless self-service support. Simultaneously, they want to drive revenue growth through dynamic pricing, targeted marketing, and personalized product suggestions. 
The seamless integration of AI-powered tools into their existing Google Cloud environment will enable ACME Corp to efficiently manage their vast media library, enhance user experiences, and unlock new revenue streams. Google Cloud’s generative AI will solidify their leadership in the media industry. 
Existing Technical Environment 
ACME Corp's content management and delivery platform leverages on compute for scalability and high availability, essential for handling their vast media library. Their extensive media library, spanning various documents, audio and video formats, is stored in Cloud Storage. To gain valuable insights into user behavior, content consumption patterns, and audience demographics, ACME Corp leverages BigQuery as their primary data warehouse. Additionally, they use Cloud Run functions for serverless execution of event-driven tasks such as video transcoding, metadata extraction, and personalized content recommendations.


While ACME Corp has made significant strides in cloud adoption, they also maintain some legacy on-premises systems for specific workflows like content ingestion and archival. These systems are slated for modernization and migration to Google Cloud in the near future. User management and authentication are currently handled through a combination of Google Identity and third-party identity providers. For monitoring and observability, ACME Corp relies on a mix of native Google Cloud tools like Cloud Monitoring and open-source solutions like Prometheus, with alerts primarily delivered via email notification. 
Beyond core infrastructure, ACME Corp is looking to extend their cloud modernization efforts to internal business processes. Currently, the Marketing team operates across siloed tools, creating significant bottlenecks when launching promotional campaigns, tracking content virality, and analyzing audience sentiment. Concurrently, the Talent and HR Operations teams face friction when onboarding external media creators, voice actors, and freelance journalists; managing independent contractor agreements, compliance tracking, and creator payouts remains a highly manual operational challenge. Bridging the gap between ACME's rich cloud data repositories and these front-office business workflows is a critical priority. 
Business Requirements 
* Accelerate and enhance the reliability of operational workflows across all environment [GCP + On-prem] 
* Simplify infrastructure management for rapid application deployment. 
* Optimize cloud storage costs while maintaining high availability and scalability for media content. 
* Enable natural language interaction with the platform with 24/7 user support
* Automatically generate concise summaries of media content. 
* Extract descriptive metadata from media assets using LLM. 
* Detect and filter inappropriate content
* Analyze media content to identify trends and extract insights. 
* Informed content strategy and decision-making with data. 
* Modernize business workflows to streamline operational challenges for internal teams 
Technical Requirements 
* Secure, high-performance hybrid cloud connectivity for data ingestion
* Provide scalable, performant compute environments both on-premises and in the
* cloud.
* Optimize cloud storage costs for growing media volumes. 
* Design AI-powered detection of harmful content. 
* Ensure that AI systems are auditable and their decisions can be explained 
* Leverage LLMs and conversational AI for personalized experiences and content virality. 
* Develop chatbots with natural language understanding to provide personalized assistance. 
* Automated summarization for diverse media. 
* Develop agents to automate their internal workflows and improve overall business operations
